Nestled in the scenic coastal town of Discovery Bay, St. Ann, Ocean's Edge Country Club is a premium residential development comprising 12 elegant townhouses. Designed for those who appreciate luxury and privacy, the project offers a gated community with top-tier security, a clubhouse for social gatherings, and a beautiful pool that serves as the centerpiece of the complex. The project reflects modern Caribbean architecture while providing residents with an exclusive and serene lifestyle just steps away from the ocean.
